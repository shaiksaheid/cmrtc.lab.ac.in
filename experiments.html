LIST OF EXPERIMENTS

S.N Week Task
PC Hardware:
1 Week 1 Identify the peripherals of a computer, components in a CPU and its functions. Disassemble and assemble the PC to working condition.
2 Week 2 Install operating systems (Linux/Windows) and configure dual boot.
3 Week 3 Hardware Troubleshooting: Diagnose and fix non-booting PC issues.
4 Week 4 Software Troubleshooting: Fix software-related CPU issues.
Internet & World Wide Web
5 Week 5 Connectivity Boot Camp: Configure LAN & TCP/IP, browse websites.
6 Week 6 Customize browsers, configure plug-ins like Flash, JRE, and proxy settings.
7 Week 7 Learn search engines, demonstrate use of Google, Yahoo, etc.
8 Week 8 Cyber Hygiene: Install antivirus, block popups and active x.
9 Week 9 Develop homepage using HTML with photo, details, and skill set.
Productivity Tools: Word
10 Week 10 Create project certificate in Word. Use formatting, text effects, headers, footers, etc.
11 Week 11 Spreadsheet basics: toolbars, saving files, scheduler using formulas, formatting text.
12 Week 12 PowerPoint basics: layout, inserting text, formatting, hyperlinks, tables, and charts.
